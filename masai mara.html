Masai Mara Safari Packages

3 DAYS MAASAI MARA +

3 DAYS MAASAI MARA ADVENTURE

A brief but thrilling safari experience in Kenya's most famous wildlife reserve, perfect for travelers with limited time.

Itinerary

Day 1: Nairobi to Masai Mara

Early morning, you will be picked up from your Nairobi residence, hotel, or the International Airport and embark on an exciting journey to the renowned Maasai Mara Game Reserve, passing through the vibrant Maasai town of Narok along the way. Upon arrival at your tented camp, you will be greeted with a delicious lunch that sets the stage for your adventure. Afterward, prepare for your first thrilling afternoon game drive, where you will have the opportunity to spot the majestic "BIG FIVE" and an array of other wildlife that inhabit the expansive plains. As the day winds down, enjoy a delightful dinner and retreat to your comfortable accommodations at MARA SOPA.

Day 2: Mara

Start your day with an exhilarating early morning game drive in the reserve, where the dawn light brings wildlife to life in their natural habitat, offering you the best chance to witness animals in action. After a thrilling morning of game viewing, indulge in a delightful picnic breakfast amidst the stunning scenery, soaking in the sights and sounds of nature all around you. Following this, return to your camp for a leisurely lunch, recharging for an exciting afternoon game drive that promises more wildlife encounters. As the day winds down, enjoy a delicious dinner at the MARA SOPA Lodge, where you can reflect on the day's adventures before settling in for a restful overnight stay.

Day 3: MARA-NAIROBI

As the sun begins to rise, casting a golden glow over the savannah, embark on your final early morning game drive, where the wildlife awakens and the landscape comes alive with vibrant colors and sounds. After soaking in the breathtaking views and spotting elusive animals, return to your camp for a hearty breakfast, savoring the flavors of the region while reflecting on the unforgettable experiences of your safari. Following breakfast, you'll depart for Nairobi, enjoying the scenic journey back to the city. Anticipate an afternoon arrival, where you'll be dropped off at your chosen city residence, hotel, or the International Airport, concluding your remarkable adventure in the heart of nature.

Package Includes:

  • Transportation in a safari minivan with pop-up roof
  • Services of a professional safari guide
  • 2 nights accommodation in a standard tented camp
  • Full board (all meals)
  • Game drives as per itinerary
  • Park entrance fees
  • Bottled water during game drives

Package Excludes:

  • International flights
  • Visa fees
  • Travel insurance
  • Personal expenses
  • Tips and gratuities
  • Optional activities
  • Alcoholic and soft drinks
7 DAYS SERENA SPECIAL +

7 DAYS SERENA SPECIAL

An exclusive week-long safari experience staying at the luxurious Serena lodges and exploring Kenya's most spectacular wildlife areas.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival

Upon your arrival at Nairobi Airport, Patmac warmly greets you and assists with your transfer to the luxurious Serena Hotel. As you check in, you take a moment to unwind and soak in the serene atmosphere, preparing for the adventure ahead. With your 7-day safari set to begin at Sweetwater’s at dawn, you enjoy a restful evening before settling in for a good night's sleep, eagerly anticipating the wildlife encounters that await you. Bed & breakfast Serena Hotel

Day 2: Nairobi-Sweetwater’s Serena Camp

Depart Nairobi in the morning and embark on a scenic drive northward, passing through Thika, renowned for its lush pineapple plantations. Arrive at Sweetwater’s in time for a delicious lunch, after which you can spend the afternoon exploring the conservancy, home to a variety of wildlife and stunning landscapes. In the evening, enjoy a delightful dinner at Sweetwater’s Serena Camp, where you will spend the night, all included in your full board meal plan.

Day 3: Sweetwater’s

Start your day with an exhilarating early morning game drive in the stunning Sweetwater’s Sanctuary, where you can witness the beauty of wildlife awakening to a new day. After a hearty breakfast, take some time to unwind and soak in the serene surroundings before enjoying a delicious lunch. In the afternoon, embark on another exciting game drive, which includes visits to the charming Chimp and Rhino Sanctuaries, offering a unique opportunity to learn about and observe these incredible animals up close. Spend the night at Sweetwater’s Serena Camp, where you'll enjoy full board meals, ensuring a delightful culinary experience throughout your stay.

Day 4: Lake Elementeita

After a delightful breakfast, embark on a scenic drive to Lake Elementeita, a true haven for bird enthusiasts, where you can observe around 300 different bird species in their natural habitat (265 km). Upon arrival, savor a delicious lunch before indulging in some leisure time. You might choose to take a refreshing swim in the lodge's pool or treat yourself to a soothing massage to unwind. As the day comes to a close, enjoy a cozy overnight stay at the Lake Elementeita Serena Lodge, where you'll be treated to full board meals, ensuring a delightful culinary experience throughout your stay.

Day 5 & 6: Masai Mara

After enjoying a hearty breakfast, set off for the iconic Maasai Mara, where the connection to nature deepens as you enter this renowned wildlife reserve. Experience the thrill of the safari from the comfort of our custom-built vehicles, designed for optimal viewing. Guided by our knowledgeable experts, you'll traverse the stunning landscapes, witnessing the majestic presence of elephants, lions, and cheetahs, along with a plethora of other unique wildlife that call the Mara home. Each game drive, lasting up to two hours, promises a fresh adventure, with the best wildlife sightings often occurring during the early morning or late afternoon. As the sun sets, return to the serene comfort of Serena Lodge for dinner and a restful overnight stay, all while enjoying full board meals.

Day 7: Masai Mara

After breakfast drive to Nairobi (335 km - approximately 5 hours); Arrive on time for arranged Lunch afterwards transferred to the airport for your outbound flight.

  • Meet & Greet (transfers)
  • Accommodation and meals as per the itinerary
  • Shared safari vehicle with pop up roof
  • Guaranteed window seat
  • English speaking or bi-lingual driver-guide
  • One litre drinking water per day
  • Park entry and conservation fees
  • Government taxes and VAT

Package Excludes:

  • International flights
  • Visa fees
  • Travel insurance
  • Personal expenses
  • Tips and gratuities
  • Optional activities (balloon safari, cultural visits)
  • Alcoholic and soft drinks
MIGRATION WAVE – LUXURY MARA ENKAJI +

MIGRATION WAVE – LUXURY MARA ENKAJI

Experience the breathtaking Great Migration in ultimate luxury at the exclusive Mara Enkaji camp. Witness one of nature's most spectacular events in style.

Itinerary

Day 1: Nairobi - Maasai Mara Game Reserve

In the early morning, you will be picked up from your Nairobi residence, hotel, or the International Airport and embark on a scenic drive to the renowned Maasai Mara Game Reserve, passing through the vibrant Maasai town of Narok. Upon arrival at your luxurious tented camp, a delicious lunch will be served, setting the stage for an exciting afternoon. You'll then set out on your first game drive, where you'll have the opportunity to spot the “BIG FIVE” and a variety of other wildlife that inhabit the expansive plains. After an exhilarating day, enjoy a delightful dinner and settle in for the night at Mara Enkaji, surrounded by the sounds of the African wilderness.

Day 2: Maasai Mara Game Reserve

Experience the thrill of an early morning game drive in the reserve, when the wildlife is most active and you can witness animals in their natural hunting habitat. As the sun rises, embark on this unforgettable adventure and immerse yourself in the breathtaking scenery. After a morning of exploration, savor a delicious picnic breakfast amidst the stunning backdrop of nature, watching game roam freely around you. Return to your luxurious camp for a delightful lunch, where opulence meets comfort. In the afternoon, set out for another exciting game drive, giving you more opportunities to encounter the majestic wildlife of the region. Conclude your day with a sumptuous dinner and a restful overnight stay at the exquisite MARA ENKAJI Camp, where every detail is designed to enhance your safari experience.

Day 3: Masai Mara

Experience an unforgettable full day in the Maasai Mara, staying at the luxurious Mara Enkaji camp, FULL BOARD. Begin your adventure with a breathtaking hot air balloon safari at dawn, soaring over the stunning savannah and witnessing the wildlife from above. After landing, enjoy a delightful bush breakfast amidst the picturesque landscape. In the afternoon, embark on a guided nature walk led by knowledgeable local guides, where you can explore the diverse flora and fauna up close. This immersive experience will deepen your appreciation for the Maasai Mara’s natural beauty and rich wildlife, making for a truly memorable day.

Day 4: Maasai Mara Game Reserve - Nairobi

After enjoying a hearty breakfast at Enkaji, depart from Mara and make your way to the capital city for your scheduled drop-off. The journey will take you through scenic landscapes, allowing you to soak in the beauty of the surroundings before reaching your destination.

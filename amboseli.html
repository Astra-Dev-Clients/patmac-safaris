6 NIGHTS WILDERNESS SKY EXPERIENCE +

6 NIGHTS WILDERNESS SKY EXPERIENCE

A comprehensive safari experience combining Amboseli's majestic elephants with the wildlife-rich Masai Mara.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Nairobi

Upon your arrival at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport, you will be warmly greeted by our representative and experienced safari driver guide. They will assist you with your luggage and ensure a smooth transfer to your city hotel, where you can unwind and relax after your journey. Enjoy the amenities and comfort of the Serena Hotel, your home for the night, as you prepare for the exciting adventures that lie ahead. Overnight stay at SerenaHotel. Meal Plan: Bed & Breakfast

Day 2: Nairobi – Fly to Amboseli National Park

After being picked up from your hotel, you'll be transferred to Wilson Airport for your morning flight to Amboseli National Park, which takes about an hour. Upon arrival, a lodge vehicle will be waiting to take you to the Amboseli Serena Safari Lodge. Once settled in, you will embark on an exciting game drive, where you can marvel at the stunning landscapes and witness the majestic herds of elephants that the park is famous for. In the evening, return to the lodge for delicious meals and a comfortable overnight stay, surrounded by the natural beauty of Amboseli. Meal Plan: Full Board

Day 3: Amboseli National Park

Start your day with a hearty breakfast while taking in the breathtaking views of Kilimanjaro during an early morning game drive. Experience the thrill of spotting wildlife, including zebras, giraffes, and cheetahs, against the stunning backdrop of the mountain. As the sun sets, indulge in a delightful bush barbeque dinner under the stars, surrounded by the sounds of nature and a variety of birds. Conclude your adventurous day with a comfortable overnight stay at the Amboseli Serena lodge, where you can relax and reflect on the day's unforgettable experiences.

Day 4: Amboseli – Fly to Masai Mara Game Reserve

After enjoying a hearty breakfast, you will be transferred to Amboseli airstrip for your morning flight to the renowned Masai Mara Game Reserve, ensuring a timely arrival. Once you land in Masai Mara, a warm welcome awaits as you are picked up from the airstrip and taken to your luxurious camp. In the afternoon, prepare for exhilarating game drives in the reserve, where you will have the opportunity to spot the majestic Big Five—lions, elephants, buffaloes, leopards, and rhinoceroses—roaming freely in their natural habitat. After an exciting day of wildlife viewing, you will savor delicious meals and unwind with an overnight stay at the Mara Serena Safari Camp, surrounded by the beauty of the African wilderness. Meal Plan: Full Board

Day 5 & 6: Masai Mara Game Reserve

Experience full days in the breathtaking Masai Mara Game Reserve, renowned for its incredible wildlife diversity and spectacular game viewing opportunities year-round. This iconic destination is a must-visit for any safari enthusiast, particularly during the awe-inspiring wildebeest migration and zebra crossings from the Serengeti between June and October. Enjoy a unique breakfast experience surrounded by the stunning landscapes and rich biodiversity of the Reserve. After thrilling days of exploration, retreat to the comfort of the Mara Serena Safari Camp for delicious meals and restful overnight accommodations, ensuring an unforgettable adventure in one of Africa's premier wildlife sanctuaries. Meal Plan: Full Board

Day 7: Maasai Mara National Reserve - Nairobi

After breakfast, you will be transferred to the airstrip in Masai Mara for your scheduled flight to Nairobi. Upon arrival, pick up from Wilson airport and transfer to Jomo Kenyatta International Airport for your out bound flight.

  • Park entrance fees

  • Internal flights as specified

Experience the breathtaking Great Migration in ultimate luxury at the exclusive Mara Enkaji camp. Witness one of nature's most spectacular events in style.

Day 2: Great Migration Experience

Early breakfast followed by a full day tracking the wildebeest migration. Witness river crossings (seasonal) and abundant predator activity. Gourmet picnic lunch in the bush. Evening game drive back to camp. Special dinner under the stars and overnight.

Morning game drive focusing on big cats. Return to camp for brunch. Afternoon visit to an authentic Maasai village with cultural experiences. Late afternoon game drive. Dinner and overnight at the camp.

Day 4: Bush Walk and Balloon Safari

Optional dawn hot air balloon safari (included). Champagne breakfast upon landing. Return to camp for relaxation. Guided bush walk with an armed ranger and Maasai guide. Evening game drive. Farewell dinner and overnight.

Day 5: Masai Mara to Nairobi

Final morning game drive. Breakfast at the camp. Flight back to Nairobi's Wilson Airport. Transfer to your hotel or international airport.
